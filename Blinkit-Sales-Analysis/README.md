**6. Key KPIs Tracked:**

- **Total Sales:** Overall revenue generated across all outlets and products
- **Average Sales per Item:** Average revenue contribution per items sold
- **Number of Items Sold:** Total count of items contributing to sales volume
- **Average Customer Rating:** Overall customer satisfaction rating across different outlets

**7. Dashboard Analysis:**

- Analyzes overall sales performance using key KPIs such as total sales, average sales per item, number of items sold, and average customer rating.
- Examines sales distribution across outlet sizes (Small, Medium, High) to understand performance variation by outlet scale.
- Breaks down sales by item categories to assess category-level contribution to overall revenue.
- Analyzes product performance based on fat content (Low Fat vs Regular) to compare category-level demand patterns.
- Evaluates sales contribution across outlet location tiers (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3) to compare geographical performance.
- Compares performance across different outlet types (Supermarket Type 1, Supermarket Type 2, Supermarket Type 3, Grocery Store).
- Provides interactive filtering options to dynamically analyze data by outlet size, location type, and item category.
- Tracks sales trends across outlet establishment years to study performance over time.

**8. Key Insights Generated**
- Medium-sized outlets contribute a significant share of total sales, indicating an optimal balance between scale and operational efficiency.
- Tier 2 locations generate higher sales compared to Tier 1 and Tier 3, highlighting strong demand potential in emerging urban markets.
- Low-fat products contribute a higher share of sales compared to regular products, reflecting increasing preference toward healthier options.
- Outlets established in recent years show strong sales performance, indicating effective expansion strategies and growing customer adoption.
- Average customer ratings remain consistently high across outlet types, suggesting uniform service quality and customer experience.
- Supermarket formats outperform grocery stores in terms of overall sales and item visibility, indicating higher efficiency in organized retail outlets.

**9. Business Questions Answered**
- How do sales vary across different outlet sizes and location tiers?
- Which product categories drive maximum sales volume?
- How does fat content impact product demand and sales performance?
- How do customer ratings vary across outlet types?
- How have sales trends evolved over outlet establishment years?

**10. Business Value**

This dashboard enables decision-makers to:
- Monitor sales performance quickly using high-level KPIs
- Identify high-performing outlet sizes and locations for expansion planning
- Optimize product mix and inventory allocation based on demand patterns
- Support data-driven strategic decisions using historical sales trends
- Improve operational planning through outlet-level performance comparison
