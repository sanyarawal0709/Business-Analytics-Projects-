**1. Adidas Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard**

This project presents an interactive **Adidas Sales Analysis dashboard** developed using **Power BI**.  
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, and customer demand across regions, retailers, products, and time periods.

**2. Objective:**
To analyze Adidas sales data and deliver **KPI-driven insights** that help identify high-performing regions, top retailers, best-selling product categories, and monthly sales trends

**3. Tools & Technologies**
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Data Modeling  
- Data Visualization  
- Business Analytics

- **Operating Profit**

- **Average Price per Unit**
- **Average Margin (%)**

**6. Dashboard Analysis**

**-Sales by Retailer**
- Comparative analysis of major retailers such as **West Gear, Foot Locker, Sports Direct, Amazon, Walmart, and Kohl’s**
- Highlights top revenue-generating retail partners

 **-Sales by Product Category**
- Breakdown of sales across:
  - Men’s Street Footwear  
  - Women’s Apparel  
  - Men’s Athletic Footwear  
  - Women’s Street Footwear  
  - Men’s & Women’s Apparel  
- Enables identification of **best-selling product segments**

**-Sales by Region**
- Regional contribution analysis across **West, Northeast, Southeast, South, and Midwest**
- Visual representation of region-wise sales share

**-Monthly Sales Trend**
- Time-series analysis of monthly sales performance
- Helps identify **seasonality, peaks, and demand fluctuations**

**7. Interactive Features**
- **Region slicer** for region-specific analysis
- **Invoice Date filter** to analyze sales trends over selected time periods
- Dynamic visuals that update based on filter selections

**8. Business Questions Answered**
- Which regions contribute the highest share of Adidas sales?
- Which retailers generate maximum revenue?
- Which product categories perform best in terms of sales and profitability?
- How do sales trends vary month-by-month?
