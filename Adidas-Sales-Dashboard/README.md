**Power BI Dashboard Showcase**

**Adidas Sales Analysis**

**1. Project Overview**

This project analyzes Adidas sales data to evaluate business performance across regions, products, and sales channels.  
The interactive Power BI dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights to support strategic decision-making in sales and marketing.

**2. Business Objective:**

To help stakeholders:
- Track overall sales and profitability
- Identify high-performing regions and products
- Understand channel-wise and retailer-wise performance
- Detect sales trends and patterns over time

**3. Tools & Technologies**

MS Excel,
Power BI,
DAX,
Data Modeling,
Data Visualization,
Business Analytics

**6. Key KPIs Displayed**

Total Sales Revenue,
Operating Profit,
Total Units Sold,
Average Price per Unit,
Average Margin (%),
Product-wise Contribution to Revenue

**7. Dashboard Analysis**

**Sales by Retailer:**

Comparative analysis of major retailers such as West Gear, Foot Locker, Sports Direct, Amazon, Walmart, and Kohl’s
Highlights top revenue-generating retail partners.

**Sales by Product Category:**

Breakdown of sales across:

Men’s Street Footwear,
Women’s Apparel,
Men’s Athletic Footwear,
Women’s Street Footwear,
Men’s Apparel,
Men’s Athletic Footwear

**Sales by Region:**

Regional contribution analysis across West, Northeast, Southeast, South, and Midwest

**Monthly Sales Trend**

Time-series analysis of monthly sales performance helps identify seasonality, peaks, and demand fluctuations

**8. Key Insights Generated**
- Certain regions consistently outperform others, contributing the highest share of total revenue
- Specific product categories drive a major portion of sales and profit
- Sales performance varies significantly across different sales channels and retailers
- Seasonal trends indicate peak sales periods that can be leveraged for promotions and inventory planning

**9. Business Questions Answered**
- Which regions contribute the highest share of Adidas sales?
- Which retailers generate maximum revenue?
- Which product categories perform best in terms of sales and profitability?
- How do sales trends vary month-by-month?

**10. Business Value**

This dashboard enables decision-makers to:
- Monitor sales performance at a glance
- Identify revenue-driving regions and products
- Optimize marketing and sales strategies
- Improve data-driven planning and forecasting
